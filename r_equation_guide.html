Equation Guide
  • \(N\) — population size at time-step \(t\)
  • \(r_d\) — discrete growth factor
    • \(r = 0\): no growth
    • \(r > 0\): positive growth
    • \(r < 0\): negative growth (decline)
  • \(K\) — carrying capacity (maximum sustainable population)
Limit Cycles and Chaos

At \(N = 10\) and \(K = 1000\):

Growth rate Behaviour
\(r_d < 2.000\) Damping oscillations toward \(K\)
\(2.000 < r_d < 2.449\) Two-point stable limit cycle
\(2.449 < r_d < 2.570\) Geometric increase in limit-cycle period (2, 4, 8, 16 …)
\(r_d > 2.570\) Aperiodic (chaotic) dynamics
