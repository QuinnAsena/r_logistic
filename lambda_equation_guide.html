Equation Guide
  • \(N\) — population size at time-step \(t\)
  • \(\lambda\) — finite rate of increase
    • \(\lambda \gtrapprox 1.02\): positive growth (at \(N = 10\), \(K = 1000\))
    • \(\lambda \lessapprox 1.01\): negative growth (at \(N = 10\), \(K = 1000\))
    • \(\lambda = 1.03\): stable equilibrium (at \(N = 42\), \(K = 1442\))
  • \(K\) — carrying capacity (maximum sustainable population)
Limit Cycles and Chaos

At \(N = 10\) and \(K = 1000\):

Growth rate Behaviour
\(\lambda < 3\) Damping oscillations toward \(K\)
\(3 < \lambda < 3.4\) Two-point stable limit cycle
\(3.4 < \lambda < 3.6\) Geometric increase in limit-cycle period (2, 4, 8, 16 …)
\(\lambda > 3.6\) Aperiodic (chaotic) dynamics
